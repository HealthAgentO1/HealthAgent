# Art Direction Document: Nordic Health Archive

## 1. Creative North Star: "The Summer Solstice Archive"
The objective of this visual direction is to redefine medical financial management through a lens of serenity, clarity, and editorial design. It moves away from high-density, utility-first UI and toward a calm, "human" experience that feels like a curated annual report or a high-end wellness publication. This user interface should feel healthy and connected to nature. This user interface should not feel like you are interacting with a software it should feel natural. This design should look and feel like a warm summer afternoon.

---

## 2. Visual Principles
*   **Whitespace as Architecture:** Use generous whitespace as the primary tool for hierarchy and breathing room. Never cramp elements.
*   **The Editorial Window:** Hero sections should feel like a view through a window, using full-bleed nature photography behind frosted glass.
*   **Layered Depth:** Depth is achieved through translucency and tonal layering rather than hard drop shadows.
*   **Human-Centric Precision:** Pair light-weight, modern typography with precise, thin rules and elegant alignment.

---

## 3. Color Palette: Muted Naturals
*   **Soft White (#FAFAF8):** The base page color. Like morning light on a white wall.
*   **Sage Green (#7A9E87):** The primary brand accent. Used for active states, growth indicators, and key CTAs.
*   **Warm Ivory (#F5F2EC):** Secondary surface color for cards and sidebars. Provides warmth and soft contrast.
*   **Soft Slate Blue (#8FA3B1):** Used for icons, secondary text, and subtle borders.
*   **Dark Sage (#4A7C6F):** For high-contrast data callouts and active button states.
*   **Text Hierarchy:** Primary (#2C2C2A), Secondary (#6B6B68).

---

## 4. Typography: Humanist Precision
*   **Typeface:** Inter (Humanist Sans-Serif).
*   **Weights:** 300 (Light) for large numbers and headlines; 400 (Regular) for body; 500 (Medium) for UI labels. **Avoid Bold (600+).**
*   **Styling:** Sentence case for all labels. Letter spacing should be slightly tightened for large headers and slightly tracked out for small labels.

---

## 5. UI Elements & Components
*   **Frosted Glass Cards:** 
    *   Fill: `rgba(245, 242, 236, 0.82)`
    *   Border: 1px `rgba(122, 158, 135, 0.15)`
    *   Effect: `backdrop-filter: blur(16px)`
    *   Corner Radius: 12px
*   **The Ledger (Tables):** No zebra striping. Use 0.5px solid dividers in slate blue at 15% opacity.
*   **Navigation:** Left-aligned. Active state signaled by Sage Green text and a subtle 2px left border. No background pill highlights.

---

## 6. Imagery Guidelines
*   **Style:** Documentary-style photography, not stock. Sunlight through leaves, wide horizons, or soft architectural details.
*   **Treatment:** Slightly desaturated and cool-toned. Avoid warm filters; let the ivory surfaces provide the warmth.
*   **Placement:** Fixed background at the top of the page, visible through frosted glass overlays.

---

## 7. Interaction & Motion
*   **Transitions:** Soft fades and subtle scale changes (98% to 100%).
*   **Scrolling:** Use fixed background positioning to create a sense of depth as content layers over the hero image.
